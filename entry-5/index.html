QUAZY SAGE

THE HEALER

Card information

A healer with a few loose feathers... literally. Her quacks are riddled with wisdom and laughs, but through all that, she always knows what to do. She brings a mix of chaos and calm to the battlefield. Her healing ability is precise, although you can't always pinpoint how it works... all you need to know is that it just does.

Abilities

EXTRA FEATHERS

Quazy Sage lets out a flurry of rejuvenating feathers that drift towards her nearest allies as they land, they heal wounds and restore balance, healing her nearest allies for 20 hp

HATCH 100

Quazy sage channels her energy into a radiant egg of restoration. When it bursts, the shell purifies the battlefield, dealing 100 damage to enemies. In addition, she also heals all allies by 10 hp
