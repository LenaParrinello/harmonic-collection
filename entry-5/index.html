QUAZY SAGE

THE HEALER

Card Information:

A healer with a few loose feathers... literally. Her quacks are riddled with wisdom and laughs but through all that always know what to do. she brings a mix of chaos and clam to the battlefield. Her healing ability are precise although you can't always pinpoint how it works... all you need to know it that it just does.

Abilities:

EXTRA FEATHERS

Quazy sage lets out a flurry of rejuvinating feathers that drift towards her nearest allies as they land they heal wounds and restore balance, healing her nearest allies for 20 hp

HATCH 100

Quazy sage channels her energy into a radient egg of restoration when it burst the shell purifies the battlefeild, dealing 100 damage to enemies. in addition she also heals all allies by 10 hp
