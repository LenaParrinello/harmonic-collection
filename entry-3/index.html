PUDDLES

The Water Mage

Card information

Born on the quiet edge of a misty pond, Puddles, a shy duck whose heart beats like soft rain, learns to harness the strength of a soft drizzle. Though easily startled, each waddle sends soothing ripples that calm allies and unsettle foes. In battle, Puddles channels his nervous energy into quiet power, proving that even the most timid spirits can become an unstoppable force

Abilities

DRIP DROP 60

Puddles releases a gentle but unrelenting drizzle, each drip striking with quiet precision as it deals 60 damage to opponents.

WADDLE 10

Hesitantly, Puddles nervously waddles across the battlefield. He sends a small ripple that unsettles his foes, dealing 10 damage, and reduces all damage Puddles takes by 30 during your opponent's next turn. If Puddles already has any damage counters, heal 20 to show quiet resilience.
