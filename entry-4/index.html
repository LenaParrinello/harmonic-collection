DUCKSTRAUGHT

The Lighting mage

Card information

When a dark cloud forms, Duckstrough waddles in with no fear. She strides into battle with sparks. Fearless and unyielding, she channels the sky's fury into every strike, turning quacks into lightning storms and challengers into charred feathers

Abilities

Quackling Bolt 10

Duckstraught lets out with a sharp quack, a jittery spark from her beak deals 10 damage to her opponent and leaves them stunned for 30 seconds, leaving her opponents vulnerable to water-type attacks

Fried Chicken 50

Duckstraught summons a storm of crackling thunder and unleashes a sizzling surge that scratches everything in its path, dealing 50 damage to her opponent. If this attack knocks out her opponent, draw 1 extra card as a bonus.

Environment

Duckstraught thrives in stormy, electrically-charged environments like Lightning Lagoon. Thunderstorms and areas with high electrical activity amplify her lightning-based attacks, giving her bonus damage and faster charging times. Dark clouds, static air, and crackling energy fields energize her powers. However, in underground or heavily insulated environments where electricity cannot flow freely, her powers are significantly weakened.

Strengths & Weaknesses

Water - Strength

Duckstraught's lightning attacks are devastating against water opponents. Electricity conducts perfectly through water, dealing double damage to aquatic foes and shocking them with amplified voltage. Water types have no defense against her crackling strikes.

Ground - Weakness

Ground and earth-based opponents completely nullify Duckstraught's lightning attacks. Electricity is absorbed harmlessly into the earth, making her powers useless against ground types. She must rely on physical attacks and strategy when facing these foes.
