BUZZ RAFTOR

THE LEGENDARY DUCK

Card information

Buzz Raftor, a duck feared by all and understood by none. A legendary duck that one could only dream about becoming allies with. She comes out in battle only when absolutely necessary and never fails to accomplish what needs to get done. She is a last resort for any team she's assigned to.

Abilities

MMMH THAT'S SOME GOOD MULCH

Buzz Raftor releases a fury of razor sharp woodchips that travel faster than the speed of light, obliterating any duck that stands in her way. Deals the remaining amount of health to any chosen enemy. This attack can only be used for one round during the entire match.

Environment

Buzz Raftor is a legendary duck who transcends environmental limitations. She performs at peak power in any terrain, from Mulchy Pond to barren wastelands. Her legendary status means no environment weakens her, though she seems to prefer areas rich with wood and natural materials where her woodchip attacks have endless ammunition. Her presence alone changes the battlefield, making any location her domain.

Legendary Status

As a legendary duck, Buzz Raftor has no traditional weaknesses. Her power is absolute and her razor-sharp woodchip attack can instantly defeat any opponent. She can only be summoned once per match and her ultimate ability can only be used once, making every appearance a game-changing moment. Teams that have Buzz Raftor hold the ultimate trump card, though she only fights when the stakes are highest. Her rarity and devastating power make her the most sought-after duck in all of Feathered Fighters.
