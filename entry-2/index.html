PROFESSOR BILL

The Fire Scorcerer

Card information

Once a studious scholar of the fire runes, Professor Bill discovers his flame and ignites his own thesis. A new gift is discovered that no pond could possibly contain. Now a fire sorcerer, he wanders the land. He uses his sharp wit and scorching feathers to teach his opponents that knowledge and flaming wings are a dangerous combination

Abilities

Featherd Fireball 30

Professor Bill hurls a blazing plume of enchanted feathers at the opponent, dealing 30 damage.Optional Gambit: flip a coin if heads, this attack deals+15 damage (total 45 damage). if tails it deals only 15 damage instead.

Fire Quackers 100

Professor Bill unleashes a dazzling barrage of explosive feathers, striking with scorching precision to deal a devastating 100 damage to the opponent.

Environment

Professor Bill thrives in volcanic and scorching territories like Lava Lake. The intense heat amplifies his fire-based attacks, giving him bonus damage when battling in these blazing zones. Molten landscapes and ember-filled skies energize his flames, making him nearly unstoppable in his natural habitat. However, in damp or aquatic environments, his powers are significantly weakened.

Strengths & Weaknesses

ICE - Strength

Ice opponents melt before Professor Bill's intense heat. His fire attacks deal increased damage to frozen foes, turning their icy defenses into puddles. Blizzards and frost have no effect on his burning spirit.

WATER - Weakness

Water is Professor Bill's greatest weakness. Aquatic attacks extinguish his flames and deal increased damage. Rain, waves, and water-based abilities dampen his powers significantly, forcing him to rely on strategy rather than raw firepower in these matchups.
