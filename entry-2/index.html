PROFESSOR BILL

The Fire Scorcerer

Card information

Once a studious scholar of the fire runes, Professor Bill discovers his flame and ignites his own thesis. A new gift is discovered that no pond could possibly contain. Now a fire sorcerer, he wanders the land. He uses his sharp wit and scorching feathers to teach his opponents that knowledge and flaming wings are a dangerous combination

Fire Quackers 100
