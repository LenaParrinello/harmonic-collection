Card information

Once a lonley pond duck, Quacknosis gazed into his reflection for maybe too long and got a glimps of the infinite ripples of his mind. in a second it shatterd and exspanded his consciousness at Once now he drifts between thoughts and realities. fear by some worshiped by others Quacnosis fights not for victory but to quiet the endless quacking echoing inside his head.

Abilities

Counting Ducks

Quacknosis summons his ducklings to peck at the enemys mind, deals 60 damage. flip a coin if it lands on heads it confuses your enemy for their next turn

Going Quackers

Quacknosis inleashes a phycic chaos warping the reality of his enemy. Deals 90 damage and silences them for one turn. Confused enemys take 10 damage.
