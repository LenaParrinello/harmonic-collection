QUACKNOSIS

The Psychic

Card information

Once a lonely pond duck, Quacknosis gazed into his reflection for maybe too long and got a glimpse of the infinite ripples of his mind. In a second it shattered and expanded his consciousness at once. Now he drifts between thoughts and realities. Feared by some, worshiped by others, Quacknosis fights not for victory but to quiet the endless quacking echoing inside his head.

Abilities

Counting Ducks

Quacknosis summons his ducklings to peck at the enemy's mind, deals 60 damage. Flip a coin if it lands on heads it confuses your enemy for their next turn

Going Quackers

Quacknosis unleashes a psychic chaos warping the reality of his enemy. Deals 90 damage and silences them for one turn. Confused enemies take 10 damage.

Environment

Quacknosis thrives in reflective, mystical environments like the Pond of Reflections. Still waters, misty areas, and places of deep meditation amplify his psychic powers, allowing him to confuse enemies more effectively and deal increased mental damage. However, in chaotic, loud, or heavily populated battlefields, his concentration is broken and his psychic abilities are significantly weakened.

Strengths & Weaknesses

Fighting - Strength

Quacknosis's psychic powers dominate physical fighters. His mind attacks bypass physical defenses entirely, dealing double damage to fighting-type opponents who rely on brute strength rather than mental fortitude. Confusion effects also disable their combat techniques.

Dark/Shadow - Weakness

Dark and shadow-based opponents are immune to Quacknosis's psychic manipulation. Their minds exist in chaos and darkness that his mental powers cannot penetrate. Dark attacks deal increased damage and completely bypass his psychic defenses, forcing him to rely on his ducklings for physical combat.
